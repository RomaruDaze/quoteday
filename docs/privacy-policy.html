Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 11, 2026

QuoteDay ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

Information We Collect

  • Google account information — When you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, and profile photo from Google. We use your email address as your unique identifier.
  • Quotes you create — Any quotes you add to the app are stored in your personal account on our servers (Firebase Firestore).
  • Purchase information — If you upgrade to QuoteDay Premium, the transaction is handled entirely by Google Play. We only receive a confirmation that the purchase was successful; we never see your payment details.

How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your QuoteDay account
  • To sync your quotes across devices
  • To deliver your daily quote notification
  • To verify your Premium status

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase (Firestore). All data is transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS). Each user's data is protected by security rules that prevent any other user from accessing it.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties, except as required to operate the app (Firebase, Google Play) or as required by law.

Your Rights

You can, at any time:

  • Delete your account and all associated data by contacting us
  • Request a copy of your data
  • Revoke Google Sign-In access from your Google account settings

Children's Privacy

QuoteDay is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page.
